New cards for the upcoming event Dragon's Roar Summer Breeze Superstar.  I think the winners and losers here aren't as clear as some of the past events, but hes my stab at where they might land.

---

## Notable Picks

Three cards from this event stand out as worth paying attention to — whether for raw strength, a meaningful niche, or specific team synergy.  I don't think any of these are must have, but these seem like they could be worthy of investment depending on what you are looking for.

:card[1488] is a niche XP pick. The standout effect is her 25% EXP boost at Lv90, putting her in the same comparison as :card[1584] (15% XP + 15% drop rate). Neither is strong enough to recommend on combat merit alone — there are better options for both slots. If you're chasing XP min-maxing, she's the strongest single-source healer available; :card[1584] is more accessible since she's in the standard banner, and :card[1488]'s source isn't yet known. **Niche XP pick; only worth it if you're specifically chasing XP min-maxing.**

:card[1669] is interesting as a potential reserve. At MLB it gives the same final round 25% crit rate to the top 2 phantasma as :card[1615] does for divina. This is a bit more relevant because of the strength of :card[1401], and could allow you to either crit cap :card[1401] or swap in :card[549] if you had been using :card[734], that seems like it could be very strong.  Its a bit niche because its likely only usable for very short fights, but this could be a boost for a lot of phatasma players in world boss.  Last month some players found some use for :card[1615], so I think :card[1669] could be at least as useful.  **Niche short-fight world boss pick; potentially an upgrade for Phantasma teams running :card[1401].**

:card[1672] is a more modern version of :card[495] — the jump from 5% crit rate to 10% crit rate is pretty significant and makes it worth consideration when compared to :card[1307] or :card[1540]. The majority of those two cards' value comes from the benefit to the top attackers anyway, and this can also cover mixed or anima teams. For multi round battles this is a strong option to trade a healer or a reserve slot. That being said there are strong alternatives in :card[1307] or :card[1540] and multi round battles probably don't need that level of optimization so it's easy enough to skip if you have either card and play divina or phantasma.  But its nice as a flexable option for story and towers. Her source isn't confirmed yet, but I'd speculate she's the event ranking reward. If that pans out it shifts things quite a bit — :card[495] was an LJS card, so picking up a straight modern upgrade just for ranking would make :card[1672] excellent value and well worth pushing for. Bear in mind the last event (Alice) ran fairly high cutoffs, so if she is a ranking reward expect to put in at least that much effort to secure her. **Flexible reserve for story and towers, especially for mixed or Anima teams — easy to skip if you have :card[1307] or :card[1540], but if she turns out to be the event ranking reward she's well worth pushing for as a far more accessible modern :card[495].**

## Other Cards

Sources for most of these aren't yet known, so they're grouped together for now — we'll sort by source once those are confirmed.

:card[1634] is a niche greedy assist — 40% self DMG plus 15 levels. The level half is the interesting part: ATK from levels multiplies outside every modifier stack so it doesn't get diluted by saturated team buffs the way additive Skill DMG / Crit DMG / DMG% buffs do. At very high saturation it closes some ground on :card[1444] (~10% gap for strong teams, wider for typical builds), but :card[707] from auction is the better stopgap when available and :card[1444]'s auction returns in ~Aug 2026.  It also strictly outclasses the other level-boost assists — :card[727] shares the same 15 levels but brings no secondary effect, and :card[710] only gives 10 levels, so :card[1634] ties or beats both on the part that actually matters while keeping its 40% DMG on top. The catch is the copy cost: it takes 3 copies just to bring the first ability online, a steep ask for a +40% DMG buff a single :card[696] from auction already covers at much lower cost — and the level boost that makes the card worthwhile costs more still.  I'm not sure that teams are strong enough for it to become usable, and if they do it may be replaced before we get there, so this one's very interesting but I'm not sure I can recommend.  **Potential Niche pick for very high-end saturated teams — worth running your own numbers; newer players shouldn't invest, and most others should wait for :card[1444] or :card[707] in auction.**

:card[1666] provides 20% DR to all melee allies. You generally don't want to be running a lot of melee so it won't see broad use, but 20% DR across a front line can be useful situationally, and the ability comes online at just 2 copies. If it turns out to be an event card or available for mochi that makes it a mildly interesting pickup — and the single ability makes an event/mochi source fairly plausible. Not enough to invest in, though. **Situationally useful front-line DR — a cheap grab if it's an event/mochi card (ability at 2 copies), but not worth investment.**

:card[1667] is an interesting melee card that kind of mirrors :card[1489] and some of the other Wild cards from events, but it's easily the strongest of the bunch with 40% crit rate, HP restoration and 30% DR. If you can reliably proc the conditional quickly that is pretty good for new players or for any situation where you really need a tank, and are ok losing out on team buffs that you could be getting from that slot and only requires 1 copy. **Worth picking up if it's from the event** — strongest [wild]-style tank in the batch.

:card[1668] has :card[747] built in at 2 copies. Dispels so far don't seem to be too vital, if that were to change then it would be an ok way to slot that into a slot that is less key to your team. Probably not worth investment, similar melee cards are generally much stronger and the dmg amp is a bit low and fixed due to being from an ability. **Pass; similar melee cards are stronger and dispels aren't critical yet.**

:card[1670] is a very strong auto attacker — 50% Crit Rate and 50% Crit Damage stacked on her stats make her hit hard. Her weak point is the skill: Divine Whirlwind Kick splits its damage across 2 targets and skips the damage amp that makes :card[1401]'s skill so strong, so you give up a fair bit of skill-cast damage choosing her over :card[1401]. How much she's worth really depends on her source, which isn't confirmed yet. If she turns out to be a gacha card she's a skip — not worth chasing copies of a card that's outclassed on skill. If she's an event card you can pick up through ranking or mochi she's a decent grab instead, which seems plausible since she was a half-ranking card in Ayakashi. At the half-ranking tier she's a good pickup, though MLB is a stretch for newer players and topping up copies for mochi on auction will be a long wait. **Strong auto attacker but a placeholder behind :card[1401]; skip if she's a gacha card, a decent pickup if she's an event ranking/mochi card — MLB a stretch for newer players.**

:card[1660] is 17% DR which is weaker than :card[1062] so it's probably better to wait for him to come to auction if you want to have a self DR card. **Wait for :card[1062] on auction instead.**

:card[1662] is 6% HP regen per 5, this is not a critical niche, but it will probably be a relatively cheap card to pickup if you want something on hand. **Grab if cheap; non-critical niche.**

:card[1664] is 12% lifesteal, also not a critical niche, if you want a copy and it's cheap go for it. **Grab if cheap; non-critical niche.**

:card[1665] is 43% skill damage, skill damage is not as common so this can be a decent damage increase for newer players if you find enough copies, but I wouldn't specifically target it. **Decent skill damage for newer players if cheap; don't specifically target.**

:card[1673] is a skip, there are stronger crit damage reserves and it's become a more common niche, look for 40+% before investing and only for newer players who don't already have reserves. **Skip; look for 40%+ crit damage reserves instead.**

## My Picks

Personally I'm eyeing :card[1634], :card[1669], and :card[1672] for my picks.

I want at least a copy of all three, but for actual investment my plan is to focus on :card[1669] for world boss and wait on :card[1634] and :card[1672]. :card[1669] is a solid upgrade to my world boss team since :card[1401] is my carry. :card[1634] can't really find a slot in my team now, but its a different direction to go for team building and if I end up with it close to unlocking I'm interested in trying to build with it, but I don't play to invest heavily. :card[1672] is a nice flexible option for story and towers, again not a huge increase but the it gives me most of :card[1307] and :card[739] my phantasma reserves in a single card slot, if its a ranking card I'm all in. For my teams I'd rank them :card[1669] > :card[1672] > :card[1634] — but that's just for me and my teams.
