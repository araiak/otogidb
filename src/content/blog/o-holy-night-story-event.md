## Limited Time: Half-Price 5-Star Orb

There's a **5-star bonus orb available at half price** until the event ends! Given the weaker rewards in this event, it's highly recommended that players who can [PVP farm](/blog/pvp-farming-guide) make an effort to grab this extra orb before time runs out.

---

## New Player Priority

**Aim for Chapter 4!** I think :card[1436] is the standout card for new players in this event - a solid tank, much tankier than last event's :card[1489]. If you already have strong melee units, you can skip. Later on she'll likely be replaced by units with better support skills (e.g. :card[738] has less EHP but offers more team value with good healers). If you can't reach the Chapter 4 boss, I'd recommend saving your resources (revives, items) rather than spending them on earlier chapters.

### Rating System

| Rating | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| ***** | Very good for new players |
| **** | Situational or may be replaced by other cards fairly easily |
| *** | Ok fallback if you have extra resources or need it to progress |
| * - ** | Probably not worth buying |

---

## Ranking Rewards

:card[1439] is a ranking reward and uh... yeah nothing really comes together here, tanky damage leeching ranged... maybe freeze could be something, but if you wanted freeze its likely other freeze cards that are just straight up better would be coming through the Christmas Auction (assuming there is one, see :card[383]).

:card[1438] is the overall ranking reward, and is quite good. Big progressive boost and a little defensive help with a high single-target damage skill that debuffs enemies to take more damage. She's not game breaking, but she's pretty good. I wrote my last info assuming she required 5 copies from gacha, but if you're ranking fairly high (top 200-300), she's a lot better than I originally thought.

---

## Gacha Cards

**New players: skip these.** The copies required to unlock useful abilities aren't worth the pulls.

:card[1437] is still good for a defensive assist.

:card[1992] is still fine, but there are other alternatives. I rated her assuming she was a ranking reward, being a gacha card drops her down a bit, since :card[655] is also dirt cheap although she's more single target than team buffing, but at 5 copies for the team buff big pass from me.

---

## TL;DR

**New players:** Grab :card[1436] from Chapter 4 if you need a tank, skip the rest unless you have spare currency.

---

*A note on recommendations: Play how you like! If you want a card, go for it. My recommendations highlight cards I see as standouts for newer players - ones that will help you progress. The game is fairly generous, so you can pick up cards that aren't super strong and still have resources for the next event. Don't hesitate to buy a card you like!*

## ランキング報酬

:card[1439]はランキング報酬ですが、うーん...まとまりがないですね。硬くてダメージ吸収するレンジド...フリーズが何かになるかもしれませんが、フリーズが欲しいならクリスマスオークションで来るであろうもっと良いフリーズカードの方が良いでしょう（オークションがあれば、:card[383]参照）。

:card[1438]は総合ランキング報酬で、かなり良いです。大きな累積ブーストと少しの防御支援、そして敵により多くのダメージを受けさせるデバフ付きの高単体ダメージスキル。ゲームを変えるほどではありませんが、かなり良い。以前はガチャで5コピー必要だと思って書きましたが、かなり上位（トップ200-300）にランクインしているなら、最初に思っていたよりずっと良いカードです。

## ガチャカード

**新規プレイヤー：スキップしてください。** 有用なアビリティを解放するために必要なコピー数は、ガチャを引く価値がありません。

:card[1437]は防御アシストとしてはまだ良いです。

:card[1992]はまだ悪くないですが、他の選択肢があります。ランキング報酬だと思って評価しましたが、ガチャカードなので価値が下がります。:card[655]も非常に安価ですが、チームバフというよりシングルターゲット寄りで、チームバフには5コピー必要なので私は大きくパスです。

**新規プレイヤー：** タンクが必要ならチャプター4の:card[1436]を取りましょう。余分な通貨がない限り、他はスキップで。

## 랭킹 보상

:card[1439]는 랭킹 보상인데 음... 네 뭔가 조합이 안 맞습니다. 탱키하고 데미지 흡수하는 원거리... 빙결이 뭔가 될 수도 있지만, 빙결을 원한다면 크리스마스 경매에서 올 더 좋은 빙결 카드들이 있을 겁니다(경매가 있다면, :card[383] 참조).

:card[1438]은 종합 랭킹 보상이고 꽤 좋습니다. 큰 누적 부스트와 약간의 방어 도움, 그리고 적이 더 많은 피해를 받게 하는 디버프가 달린 높은 단일 타겟 데미지 스킬. 게임을 바꿀 정도는 아니지만 꽤 좋습니다. 이전에는 가챠에서 5장이 필요하다고 생각하고 썼는데, 꽤 높은 순위(상위 200-300)에 있다면 처음 생각했던 것보다 훨씬 좋은 카드입니다.

## 排名獎勵

:card[1439] 是排名獎勵，呃...是的，這裡什麼都湊不到一起，坦克型吸血遠程...也許冰凍能有點用，但如果你想要冰凍，很可能通過聖誕拍賣會有更好的冰凍卡（假設有拍賣的話，參見 :card[383]）。

:card[1438] 是總排名獎勵，相當不錯。大量累積增益和一點防禦幫助，加上一個高單體傷害技能，能讓敵人受到更多傷害。不算改變遊戲規則，但相當不錯。我之前寫的時候假設這需要從抽卡獲得5張，但如果你排名相當高（前200-300），這張卡比我最初想的要好得多。

## Recompensas de Ranking

:card[1439] es una recompensa de ranking y uh... sí, nada encaja aquí realmente, ranged resistente que roba daño... quizás el congelamiento podría servir, pero si quieres congelamiento es probable que otras cartas de congelamiento mejores lleguen a través de la Subasta de Navidad (asumiendo que haya una, ver :card[383]).

:card[1438] es la recompensa de ranking general, y es bastante buena. Gran impulso progresivo y un poco de ayuda defensiva con una habilidad de alto daño a un objetivo que debuffea a los enemigos para que reciban más daño. No cambia el juego, pero es bastante buena. Escribí mi última información asumiendo que esto requería 5 copias de gacha, pero si estás rankeando bastante alto (top 200-300), esta carta es mucho mejor de lo que pensé originalmente.
